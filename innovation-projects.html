Artificial Intelligence Solution for the optimization of energy management operational processes

OPERATO is a project that seeks to develop an intelligent prototype based on machine learning technologies that meets the requirements of automation and intelligent treatment of electrical distribution data, being focused on the optimization operational processes in energy management.

This project is taking place in León, its execution began on 01/01/2019 and will end on 12/31/2020, its budget is 409,839 euros. This action (IDI-20181264) is co-funded by CDTI and FEDER Funds.
