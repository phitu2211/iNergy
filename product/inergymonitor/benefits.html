Information request
Smarkia Monitor is the cloud tool for monitoring energy. It connects through Internet directly to the energy meters to analyze all the consumption data with the goal of obtaining the maximum energy savings.

Benefits of using Smarkia Monitor

Monitoring energy consumption

Ongoing information, monitoring and control of energy consumption (electric, water, steam, gasoline, and natural gas consumption …), of installations, allowing detailed analysis and making possible the unified management of many sites.

Savings of costs and time

Access easily and safely consumption, energy variables, indicators and monitoring thanks to remote metering.

Low implementation cost
Smarkia Monitor allows beginning by measuring the primary consumption and expand the measurement points as necessary. You will pay only what is really needed. The Smarkia remote metering software was designed to manage millions of meters, with maximum market scalability.
Automation of processes
Smarkia Monitor reports disparities produced on the consumption foreseen, the baseline calculation, which allows detecting possible failures or losses of performance that may derive from higher consumption. In addition, it has functionalities such as Favorites, Alerts or Task management with access privileges by user, work center, installation or country.
Imputation of costs
Smarkia Monitor allows assigning the costs of energy consumption by work center, production line, installation or unit produced providing the generation of indicators of efficiency and competitiveness.
Flexibility in the generation of reports
Possibility of using models of the organization’s own reports and integrate them easily with Smarkia without the need for development, in addition, being able to generate reports totally configurable by the user.
Accessible from any place and at any time
Through different types of devices with internet access able to share the information easily within the organization.
Fast implementation
Smarkia requires no specific infrastructure to obtain the consumption of the organization since, being a Cloud solution, the need to acquire and install hardware or software licenses is eliminated, is a multiprotocol and multi-manufacturer solution.
Reduction of environmental impact
The increase in the concentration of CO2 is producing negative effects in our environment. Controlling and reducing CO2 emissions is a priority for all. Smarkia Monitor facilitates the decrease in energy consumption and reduce the emissions of CO2 and other greenhouse gases.
Security
Smarkia is a platform designed to offer maximum security in all the information management processes, at the level of access, data encryption, backup, etc.
