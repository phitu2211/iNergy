Monitoring energy

Integration of remote metering

Smarkia Monitor identify and individually monitor energy installations; they connect through Internet directly to the energy meters to analyze all the consumption data (electric, water, gas, gasoline, etc.). No additional tools are required to have access to the consumption and variables that affect the organization, since Smarkia Monitor allows connecting to the meters of different manufacturers.

Multiprotocol

Choose what type of measurement equipment you want to read, either consumption or variable energy. Obtain measurements of electricity, gas, water, heat, temperature, and production level through: Standard protocols such as IEC-870, DLMS, MODBUS, M-Bus, PRIME. Proprietary protocols such as ION of Schneider. Integration with SCADA or BMS tools through standards such as BACnet or OPC.

Multimanufacturer

Smarkia has maximum flexibility to connect to the meters of different manufacturers such as Schneider Electric, Siemens, Landis+Gyr, ZIV, Kromschroeder, Circutor..., for monitoring energy. SMARKIA does not impose restrictions or require the installation of additional infrastructure.

Visualization and Exportation of data.

Smarkia Monitor allows displaying and graphically comparing energy consumption data, variables and indicators and personalized exportation of information.

Configurable Indicators

These allow automatically establishing a relationship between energy and the variables that influence consumption.

Baseline Calculation

Smarkia allows automating the baseline calculation using variables that affect use and/or consumption to predict future consumption and calculate energy savings, allowing improving the energy budget and knowing in detail the reason for any disparities.

Management of configurable alerts

Smarkia Monitor allows defining different type of alerts that may be assigned easily to other members of the organization or collaborators.

Generation of reports

Smarkia Monitor has a flexible system for simple creation of reports that provide a valuable consumption analysis, being able to plan its generation and sending. In addition, it allows simply integrating the existing client reports (in Excel format) with the platform.

Management of documents

Archiving documents (bills, external reports, etc.) in different formats with advanced search by files or contents.

Smarkia smart billing

Rate optimizer

Possibility of simulating and compare energy bills with different powers and rates (3 periods, 6 periods). You may also simulate different consumption scenarios to calculate its optimal rate.

Optimization of power

With this functionality you may calculate the most advantageous power to contract according to its real consumption. Obtain important savings most simply.

Electric pre-billing

Possibility of generating your own billing on the 1st day of each month being able to predict and quote with that issued by the marketer considering all the parameters that affect it.

Includes generation of bills with the market rate per hour indexed from the updated prices published by the OMIE.

The Smarkia smart billing also analyzes its data and informs you of any changes or disparities produced in its consumption. With this information, you can detect any problem immediately and correct it.
