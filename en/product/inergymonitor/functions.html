Monitoring energy

Cross-platform integration and work on multi-procotol

iNergy Monitor is capable of connecting to many systems of equipment, electronic watches of different brands. Users can monitor and analyze via the Internet or company intranet. No tools or additional software required to be installed on user devices (phones, computers, laptops, tablets, etc.) can be easily accessed and monitored in real time.

Multi-protocol

Choose what type of measurement equipment you want to read, either consumption or variable energy. Obtain measurements of electricity, gas, water, heat, temperature, and production level through: Standard protocols such as IEC-870, DLMS, MODBUS, M-Bus, PRIME. Proprietary protocols such as ION of Schneider. Integration with SCADA or BMS tools through standards such as BACnet or OPC.

Multi-platform property

iNergy has maximum flexibility to connect to the meters of different manufacturers such as Schneider Electric, Siemens, Landis+Gyr, ZIV, Kromschroeder, Circutor..., for monitoring energy.

Visualization and Exportation of data.

iNERGY Monitor allows displaying and graphically comparing energy consumption data, variables and indicators and personalized exportation of information.

Configurable Indicators.

These allow automatically establishing a relationship between energy and the variables that influence consumption.

Baseline Calculation.

iNergy Monitor allows automating the baseline calculation using variables that affect use and/or consumption to predict future consumption and calculate energy savings, allowing improving the energy budget and knowing in detail the reason for any disparities.

Management of configurable alerts.

iNergy Monitor allows defining different type of alerts that may be assigned easily to other members of the organization or collaborators.

Generation of reports.

iNergy Monitor has a flexible system for simple creation of reports that provide a valuable consumption analysis, being able to plan its generation and sending. In addition, it allows simply integrating the existing client reports (in Excel format) with the platform.

Smart operation.

Includes billing with established electricity purchase price then help compare with electricity bills.

iNergy also analyzes the past data and informs you of any changes or discrepancies made in consumption. With this information, you can spot problem analysis and come up with preventive remedies.

Rate optimizer

iNergy helps you to analyze and calculate operating indicators such as pressure setting index, temperature, ... to create the most energy saving while ensuring quality for production.

Optimization of power

With the ability to simulate and compare different energy consumption scenarios to optimize the cost of energy use. The user can simulate different consumption scenarios (peak, off-peak, normal rates of use) to calculate optimum energy use.

Optimizing energy costs

iNergy provides tools and energy consumption reporting to help managers analyze causes and remedy solutions to avoid future losses and optimize energy costs.
