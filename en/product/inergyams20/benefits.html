Benefits of using iNERGY AMS20

iNERGY AMS20 is an Advanced Energy Management software designed and built by Energy Management professionals based on ISO 50001. iNergy AMS20 provides powerful tools to automate and simplify management. energy management of energy-using facilities with the goal "Energy Saving - Emissions Reducion – Environmental Protection".

Manage your energy use numbers

iNERGY AMS20 provides a digital platform for energy management systems for businesses in the era of industrial revolution 4.0

Energy saving policies

iNERGY AMS20 is a tool that facilitates the implementation of energy saving policies for the entire organization..

Low implementation cost
iNergy Ams20 allows users to simply invest in software without any additional hardware investment.
Ongoing information
Ongoing information, monitoring and control of consumption (electric, water, steam, gasoline, natural gas ...) and of the many factors that affect it.
Energy optimization plan
Ease of creation of energy optimization plan, monitoring and later dissemination among members of the organization.
Analysis of the EnPI index
iNERGY AMS20 provides a powerful analysis of the energy efficiency index (EnPI). The system automatically detects the discrepancies and gives a warning to fix the problem.
Compliance with ISO 50001
iNERGY AMS20 is designed and built based on ISO 50001 standard. It is a core element in the operation of an organization's energy management system.
Compliance with legal regulations
iNERGY AMS20 is the first Digital Energy Management System platform to help organizations meet the legal requirements for economical and efficient use of energy.
Saving energy costs
Providing energy baseline calculation tools, setting energy targets, automatically searching for energy efficiency solutions. Energy management efficiency calculations.
Automatic reports
iNergy has the ability to Possibility of using models of one’s own reports without the need for development and generation of configurable reports by the user. In addition, standard reporting templates are available.
Accessible from any place and any moment.
Through different types of devices with internet access able to share the information within the organization.
Fast implementation
Requires no specific infrastructure to obtain the consumption of the organization. iNergy is a multi-manufacturer, multi-protocol and multi-energy tool.
Reduction of environmental impact
Strengthening the continual commitment to sustainable development, reducing the emission of greenhouse gases and improving the efficient use of resources.
Security
iNergy is an all-in-one Server platform designed to provide maximum security in all information management processes, at the access level, at the level of data encryption, automatic backup, data protection and more.
