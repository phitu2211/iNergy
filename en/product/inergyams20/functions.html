Energy management efficiency with iNERGY AMS20

Cross-platform integration and work on multi-protocol

iNergy AMS20 is capable of connecting to a variety of data platforms including iNERGY Monitor, SCADA systems, BMSs or different databases within the organization. Alternatively, users can enter energy data manually or import an excel file. There is nt any additional tool or any additional software to be installed on user devices (phones, computers, laptops, tablets, etc.) which can be easily accessed and monitored anytime, anywhere.

Visualization and Exportation of data.

iNERGY AMS20 automatically analyzes the energy balance, showing the flow of energy use in an intuitive and explicit way. Users can acknowledge what kind of energy process or equipment system which uses how much mass.

Energy overview.

From the collected data, iNERGY AMS20 automatically performs energy review analyzes according to ISO 50001 including: - Analysis of key energy consumption - Analysis of area of key energy use - Calculating potential, energy saving solution - Planning and implementing energy saving solution - etc.

Establishment of objectives, goals and plans.

iNERGY AMS20 automatically calculates the potential for energy saving, thereby setting goals and targets. Automatically searches for the most appropriate energy saving and improvement opportunities for the organization, from proposals in the energy review process, thus forming improvement goals.

Performance indicators.

Automation of monitoring energy performance indicators and with alerts on any disparity, simplifying the management of non-compliance, corrective and preventive actions.

Baseline

Based on energy data and impact variables iNERGY AMS20 automatically analyzes and calculates energy baselines accurately: - Multivariate regression algorithm for 10 impact variables - Gives trends in energy use - Forecast of energy consumption in the future - Helping managers to set energy saving goals in the next period.

Task management and alert system.

Simple management of the tasks of all those involved in the energy management system of an organization.

Review by management.

All the information on the energy performance of an organization at a click. Make consultations and extract reports anytime and anywhere, and direct the future energy of its business thanks to iNERGY AMS20.

Energy audit.

iNERGY provides data and reports on energy consumption, an effective and essential tool for energy audit at the enterprise.

Calculating energy saving solutions

iNERGY automatically finds and recommends energy saving solutions based on the organization's equipment and operations profiles. Providing computational tools to analyze investment efficiency of energy saving solutions, helping management units to create profiles for implementing technical solutions to save energy.

Generation of reports.

iNergy AMS20 has a flexible system for simple creation of reports that provide a valuable consumption analysis, being able to plan its generation and sending. In addition, it allows simply integrating the existing client reports (in Excel format) with the platform.

Energy reports.

Includes billing with established electricity purchase price then help compare with electricity bills.

iNergy also analyzes the past data and informs you of any changes or discrepancies made in consumption. With this information, you can spot problem analysis and come up with preventive remedies.

Energy review report

iNergy helps you to synthesize the past energy consumption, therby the organization will have an overall review on energy, then give out direction or focus on which factors to manage.

Energy efficiency index report

iNergy helps you analyze and calculate current energy efficiency index, compare indicators with indicators at the same period last year to suggest necessary warning.

Energy management status report

iNergy provides tools and energy consumption reporting to help managers analyze causes and remedy solutions to avoid future losses and optimize energy costs.
