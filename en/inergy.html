iNERGY SYSTEM is the energy efficiency management software system consulted and designed by the most professional energy manager.

Monitor your company's energy consumption from anywhere in the world. Optimize processes and increase efficiency in productivity management and s energy saving, helping the company perform "Energy Saving - Emissions Reducion – Environmental Protection" towards green production.

You couldn’t manage what you couldn’t measure?

Do you know how much, how and where you consume your energy? Do you know how your equipment is operating, exactly when you need maintenance? Do you know where the energy problem is happening, and why? Do you know your savings potential and how to carry it out? With iNergy, you can.

The most flexible solution

With iNERGY, With Amitech you may use your existing telemetry equipment without having to invest in networks, gateways or other new equipment. You will be able to access it anytime, anywhere; manage large volumes of information simply, unifying criteria among your work centers anywhere in the world. You will share the energy efficiency solution among all the members of your organization.

More than a simple energy manager

  • Telemetry of energy consumption, variables and indicators.
  • Comparison with baselines and prediction of future consumption.
  • Information of alarms or deviations.
  • Determination of energy policies.
  • Energy review.
